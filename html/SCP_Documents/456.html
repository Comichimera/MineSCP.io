SCP Foundation

Secure, Contain, Protect

Item Number: SCP - 456

Clearance: 5 - Top Secret

Object Class: Apollyon

SCP - 456

Special Containment Procedures:

Due to SCP-456's extraterrestrial nature and ability to manifest at will, physical containment is impossible. The only means of monitoring SCP-456 is by intercepting any radio communications that may be originating from SCP-456. A specialized task force, designated Sigma-9 ("Skywatchers"), is responsible for tracking SCP-456-related activities and ensuring readiness for any manifestation events. In the event of SCP-456 communication or manifestation, the highest-ranking Foundation official available must engage with SCP-456 to fulfill their demands, with the goal of preventing the release of any pathogenic agents. All interactions with SCP-456 are to be recorded and analyzed to better understand their motives and capabilities.

Description:

SCP-456 is a race of extraterrestrial entities of unknown origin. Instances of SCP-456 are characterized by three distinct "heads" connected to a singular body via elongated necks. They stand approximately a foot taller than the average human. SCP-456 possesses the ability to communicate through radio waves but seems to understand human speech without such technology. The process by which they translate and understand human communication remains unknown.

SCP-456 has demonstrated a profound understanding of human anatomy and biology, evidenced by their capability to engineer and unleash unique strains of diseases. Historical analysis suggests SCP-456 may be responsible for various historical plagues, including the ████████ Plague, the █████ █████, and the ███████ ███.

Instances of SCP-456 have manifested on Earth, typically preceded by a column of fire descending from the sky and the appearance of a glass chamber filled with opaque green gas. Within this chamber, SCP-456 instances are barely visible and dematerialize upon departure.

Addendum 456-A: Due to the significant existential risk posed by SCP-456, all unauthorized communication with the entity is expressly prohibited. Communication with SCP-456 is to be strictly limited to designated Foundation personnel who have received the requisite clearance and training. Extreme caution is imperative during any form of interaction with SCP-456. Currently, compliance with SCP-456's demands is mandatory, as the Foundation lacks alternative contingency measures to mitigate the potential risks posed by non-compliance. The primary objective of the Foundation concerning SCP-456 has transitioned from containment to complete neutralization, reflecting the severity and uncontainable nature of the threat it presents.

Addendum 456-B: Each interaction with SCP-456 has been accompanied by progressively larger demands. There is growing concern within the Foundation that SCP-456 may soon request something beyond the Foundation's capacity to provide, potentially leading to a severe and uncontrollable plague event. Contingency plans are being developed for such a scenario, although the effectiveness of these plans against an entity of SCP-456's capabilities is uncertain.
