SCP Foundation

Secure, Contain, Protect

In the year of our Lord, under the vigilant auspices of an enlightened society dedicated to the preservation of humanity's sanctum and the safeguarding of the natural order, there emerges a fellowship of steadfast guardians and scholars, known collectively as the SCP Foundation. This esteemed conclave, born of necessity in the shadows of an ever-expanding empirical knowledge, is entrusted with a mandate both grave and grand: to Secure, Contain, and Protect.

Our mission, articulated with due gravity and unyielding resolve, is threefold. Firstly, to Secure the manifold artifacts, entities, and phenomena which, by their very nature, threaten the fabric of reality and the sanity of mankind. These anomalies, diverse in their form and inexplicable in their essence, challenge the boundaries of our understanding and the limits of our dominion.

Secondly, to Contain these specters of the unknown, ensuring that their malignant influence and potential for calamity are restrained. Within the fortified repositories of our Foundation, we employ every means of science and scholarship, every artifice wrought by man and gleaned from the arcane, to imprison these phantasmagoria. Our bulwarks stand not merely as barriers against physical escape but as bastions of secrecy, for knowledge of these entities must not permeate the collective consciousness of a populace unprepared for such revelations.

Thirdly, to Protect the unwitting masses from the terror and turmoil that knowledge and exposure to these anomalies would engender. In our custodianship lies the preservation of order, the continuation of progress, and the safeguarding of the collective human spirit against the encroaching shadows of madness and despair.

Let it be known that the SCP Foundation, guided by principles of valour, discretion, and unrelenting vigilance, stands as the bulwark against the abyss. In our quest, we are guided by the noblest of charges: to shield humanity from the unfathomable, to wrest order from chaos, and to cast light into the darkest corners of existence. We pursue this path with neither expectation of glory nor desire for public accolade, but with the steadfast commitment to duty and the unshakeable resolve to ensure the continuance of our species in the face of the unknowable.

Thus, we commit ourselves to this eternal vigil, this sacred trust. Armed with knowledge, fortified by science and sorcery alike, we face the phantasmagoria of the ages, undaunted by the spectre of the unknown and unyielding in our resolve to protect humanity at all costs. For in the balance hangs the fate of our world, entrusted to our care by both providence and necessity.
